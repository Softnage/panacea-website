Panacea Pharmaceuticals Ltd is a fully Ghanaian-owned pharmaceutical company. Established in 2007 with a single retail outlet, we have grown steadily over the years to earn a strong reputation as a leading and trusted company in the pharmaceutical retailing and distribution services in Ghana

Panacea Pharmaceuticals Ltd. currently operates a network of over 20 retail and wholesale/distribution facilities throughout Ghana.
